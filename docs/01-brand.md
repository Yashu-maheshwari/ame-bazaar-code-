
# AME Bazaar Brand Documentation

## Purpose

This document defines the official brand identity of AME Bazaar.

It serves as the single source of truth for branding, design, communication, user experience, marketing, website development, AI understanding, and future expansion.

Every future decision must remain consistent with this document.

---

# Brand Identity

## Brand Name

AME Bazaar

---

## Legal Entity

Apparel Maheshwari Enterprises

---

## Brand Type

Premium Family Fashion Retail Brand

---

## Brand Positioning

AME Bazaar is a premium family garment retailer offering quality fashion for Men, Women and Kids at fair prices.

The brand combines modern shopping experience with the trust of a local family business.

---

## Vision

To become India's most trusted family fashion retail brand with a strong offline presence, digital platform, e-commerce ecosystem, franchise network, and private label products.

---

## Mission

To provide high-quality family fashion at honest prices while delivering exceptional customer service, long-term trust, and an enjoyable shopping experience.
