# AME Bazaar Content Style Guide

## Purpose

This document defines the writing style, tone of voice, language standards, and content principles for the AME Bazaar Digital Platform.

Its purpose is to ensure every page, blog article, product description, category page, email, advertisement, and future AI-generated content maintains a consistent brand voice.

All content creators, developers, marketers, and AI systems must follow this guide.

---

# Content Objectives

Every piece of content should:

* Build trust
* Educate customers
* Answer questions
* Improve user experience
* Support SEO
* Support AI understanding
* Encourage meaningful action

Content should always prioritize helping the customer.

---

# Brand Voice

AME Bazaar should sound:

* Professional
* Friendly
* Honest
* Helpful
* Confident
* Trustworthy
* Knowledgeable
* Respectful

Never sound:

* Aggressive
* Pushy
* Clickbait
* Spammy
* Overly promotional
* Misleading

---

# Writing Style

Write in:

* Clear language
* Short sentences
* Simple words
* Active voice
* Natural conversation

Avoid unnecessary technical jargon unless it is explained.

---

# Tone

The tone should adapt to the context.

Homepage

Warm, welcoming, and trustworthy.

Category Pages

Helpful and informative.

Product Pages

Clear, descriptive, and customer-focused.

Blog Articles

Educational and practical.

Contact Page

Friendly and reassuring.

Policies

Professional and transparent.

---

# Language Standards

Use:

* Correct grammar
* Consistent terminology
* Proper punctuation
* Inclusive language

Avoid slang, excessive abbreviations, or overly casual wording on the website.

---

# Customer-Centric Writing

Focus on the customer's needs.

Explain:

* What the product is
* Why it is useful
* Who it is for
* When it should be used
* How it solves a problem

Avoid writing only about the business.

---

# Readability

Content should be easy to scan.

Use:

* Short paragraphs
* Descriptive headings
* Bullet lists
* Tables where appropriate
* Highlighted key points

Readers should find answers quickly.

---

# Calls To Action

Calls to action should be clear and helpful.

Examples:

* Explore the Collection
* Visit Our Store
* Contact Us
* Browse Categories
* Read More
* Get in Touch

Avoid manipulative phrases such as:

* Buy Now Before It's Gone
* Last Chance
* Hurry Up
* Limited Time Only

unless they are factually accurate.

---

# Product Descriptions

Every product description should explain:

* Product overview
* Key features
* Materials (when available)
* Intended use
* Care instructions
* Available sizes
* Suitable occasions

Focus on benefits as well as features.

---

# Category Content

Category pages should explain:

* What products are included
* Who the category is for
* Styling suggestions
* Seasonal recommendations
* Buying guidance

Avoid repeating the same text across multiple categories.

---

# Blog Writing

Every article should:

* Answer a real customer question
* Be well-researched
* Use logical headings
* Include practical advice
* Link to related content
* End with a helpful next step

Do not publish articles solely to target keywords.

---

# SEO Writing

Write naturally.

Use keywords only where they fit the context.

Never:

* Stuff keywords
* Repeat phrases unnaturally
* Sacrifice readability

Content should satisfy user intent before search engine requirements.

---

# AI-Friendly Writing

Structure content so AI systems can understand it easily.

Use:

* Clear headings
* Concise paragraphs
* Definitions where appropriate
* Lists
* Logical flow

Avoid ambiguous or overly complex wording.

---

# Local Content

When relevant, naturally mention:

* Kirari
* Delhi
* Nearby service areas
* Local shopping
* Community events

Only include local references when they genuinely add value.

---

# Accuracy

Only publish information that is:

* Correct
* Current
* Verifiable
* Consistent with business policies

If information changes, update existing content instead of creating conflicting pages.

---

# Content Review Checklist

Before publishing, confirm:

* The content is original.
* The information is accurate.
* The writing matches the AME Bazaar brand voice.
* The page answers the customer's question.
* The content is easy to read.
* The headings are logical.
* Internal links are included where relevant.
* Grammar and spelling have been checked.

---

# Documentation Rule

This document defines the official writing standards for the AME Bazaar Digital Platform.

Every piece of website content, blog article, product description, marketing message, and future AI-generated content must follow these guidelines to ensure consistency, clarity, trust, and a premium customer experience.
