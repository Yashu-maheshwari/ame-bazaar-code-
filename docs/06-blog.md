# AME Bazaar Blog Documentation

## Purpose

The AME Bazaar Blog is the official knowledge hub of the AME Bazaar Digital Platform.

Its purpose is to educate customers, build trust, improve search engine visibility, strengthen AI understanding, establish topical authority, and support long-term business growth.

The blog is not intended for promotional content alone. Every article should provide genuine value to readers while naturally supporting the AME Bazaar brand.

---

# Blog Objectives

The blog should:

* Build topical authority
* Improve Technical SEO
* Improve On-Page SEO
* Improve Local SEO
* Strengthen Entity SEO
* Increase organic traffic
* Support AI search visibility
* Educate customers
* Answer frequently asked questions
* Increase customer trust
* Improve internal linking
* Support future WooCommerce sales

---

# Content Philosophy

Every article should be:

* Helpful
* Accurate
* Practical
* Easy to understand
* Well-structured
* Evergreen whenever possible

Avoid:

* Thin content
* AI-generated filler
* Keyword stuffing
* Clickbait headlines
* Duplicate articles
* Misleading information

# Article Structure

Every article should follow a consistent structure.

1. Title (H1)
2. Introduction
3. Table of Contents (when appropriate)
4. Main Sections (H2)
5. Supporting Sections (H3)
6. Images
7. Helpful Tips
8. Frequently Asked Questions
9. Related Articles
10. Call To Action

---

# Writing Guidelines

Articles should:

* Use simple language
* Break long paragraphs
* Use bullet points where appropriate
* Include descriptive headings
* Answer the search intent completely
* Provide actionable advice

Write for humans first, then optimize for search engines.

---

# Internal Linking Strategy

Every article should link naturally to:

* Relevant categories
* Related blog posts
* Product pages
* About page
* Contact page
* Homepage

Internal links should improve navigation rather than manipulate rankings.

---

# Image Guidelines

Images should:

* Be high quality
* Be optimized for performance
* Include descriptive filenames
* Include meaningful alt text
* Support the article content

Avoid decorative images that do not add value.

---

# SEO Standards

Every article must include:

* Unique Title
* SEO-friendly URL
* Meta Description
* One H1
* Logical heading hierarchy
* Image optimization
* Internal links
* External references when appropriate
* Breadcrumbs
* Structured data readiness

---

# AI Readability

Every article should help AI systems understand:

* The topic being discussed
* How it relates to fashion
* How it relates to AME Bazaar
* Why the information is trustworthy
* What practical advice is being given

Content should be factual, well-organized, and supported by clear headings.
