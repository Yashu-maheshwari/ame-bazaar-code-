## 4. Complete Buying Guide
When selecting kids' clothing, look for these specific quality checkmarks:
- **Flatlock Seams**: Turn the garment inside out. Seams should lie flat to prevent rubbing against skin.
- **Waistband Adjustability**: Look for elastic waistbands with button-hole elastic strips or drawstrings to adapt to growth.
- **Reinforced Knees**: For boys' and girls' trousers, a double layer of fabric at the knees extends the garment's life.
- **Non-Toxic Dyes**: Avoid strong chemical smells, which indicate heavy chemical dyes. Opt for AZO-free dyed fabrics.

## 5. Fabric Guide

### Combed Cotton
- **Benefits**: Exceptionally soft, breathable, and durable.
- **Delhi Climate**: Perfect for dry heat and humid monsoons.
- **Care**: Machine wash in warm water with mild detergent.

### Organic Cotton
- **Benefits**: Chemical-free, hypoallergenic, and gentle on sensitive skin.
- **Delhi Climate**: Best for infants and children with eczema or skin allergies.
- **Care**: Air-dry flat to prevent shrinkage.

### Cotton-Polyester Blends (for Uniforms/Outerwear)
- **Benefits**: Durable, wrinkle-resistant, and dries quickly.
- **Delhi Climate**: Suitable for school and daily play coordinates.
- **Care**: Easy to wash and requires minimal ironing.

## 6. Size & Fit Guide

### Kids Sizing Grid (Based on Height & Age)

| Age Range | Height (cm) | Chest (Inches) | Waist (Inches) | Average Weight (kg) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **0-3 Months** | 50 - 62 cm | 16" - 17" | 15" - 16" | 3 - 5 kg |
| **6-12 Months** | 68 - 80 cm | 18" - 19" | 17" - 18" | 7 - 10 kg |
| **2-3 Years** | 92 - 98 cm | 20" - 21" | 19" - 20" | 12 - 15 kg |
| **4-5 Years** | 104 - 110 cm | 22" - 23" | 21" - 22" | 16 - 20 kg |
| **6-7 Years** | 116 - 122 cm | 24" - 25" | 22" - 23" | 21 - 25 kg |

### Fitting Mistakes to Avoid
- **Buying Too Snug**: Children grow quickly. A tight collar or chest fit will become unwearable within months.
- **Ignoring Elastic Tension**: Ensure elastic waistbands do not leave red marks on the child's skin.

## 8. Common Mistakes
- **Buying Strictly by Age Label**: Age labels are averages. Always measure your child's height first.
- **Choosing Style Over Comfort**: Buying synthetic outfits with heavy embroidery that causes scratching and skin rashes.
- **Ignoring Wash Care**: Shrinking delicate cotton coordinates by washing them in hot water.

---

## 9. Tailoring & Alteration Advice
- **Growth Alterations**: Buying trousers slightly longer and rolling the hems with a loose stitch allows them to be let down later.
- **Elastic Adjustments**: Replacing tight or worn-out elastic bands with soft, adjustable options extends garment use.
- **Timelines**: Hemming and waistband elastic replacements at Mubarakpur Road take 24 hours.

## 10. Care & Maintenance
- **Infant Clothes**: Wash separately using mild, fragrance-free detergents to protect sensitive skin.
- **Stain Pre-treatment**: Soak mud or food stains in cold water with liquid soap immediately before washing.
- **Drying**: Air dry in shade to maintain fabric softness.

## 12. Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: How do I know if my child's pants are too tight?
If the waistband leaves red marks on the skin after an hour of play, the elastic is too tight. Choose a larger size or adjust the elastic.

### Q2: What is the best fabric for children with skin allergies?
Organic, undyed combed cotton is the most skin-friendly fabric because it contains no harsh chemical residues or scratchy synthetic fibers.

### Q3: How much shrinkage should I expect in pure cotton kids' clothes?
Most raw cotton kids' clothes will shrink by about 3% to 5% after the first wash. We recommend buying one size larger to accommodate this.
