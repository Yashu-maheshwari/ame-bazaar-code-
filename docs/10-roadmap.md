# AME Bazaar Digital Platform Roadmap

## Purpose

This document defines the long-term development roadmap for the AME Bazaar Digital Platform.

It provides a structured implementation plan covering website development, SEO, AI visibility, WooCommerce integration, customer experience, automation, and future business expansion.

This roadmap should evolve as the platform grows while preserving the overall architecture.

---

# Project Vision

The goal is not simply to build a website.

The goal is to build the digital foundation for AME Bazaar's future.

The platform should become:

* Official Digital Identity
* Local Authority Website
* Knowledge Hub
* AI-readable Business Entity
* Customer Trust Platform
* WooCommerce Store
* Omnichannel Retail Platform
* Franchise Support System

---

# Guiding Principles

Every development decision should improve at least one of the following:

* User Experience
* Performance
* Accessibility
* SEO
* AI Visibility
* Conversion Rate
* Scalability
* Maintainability
* Security
